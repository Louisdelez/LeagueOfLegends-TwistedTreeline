FEATURES

Faithful recreation of the original game mode

Full 3D Map

Twisted Treeline rendered from original patch 9.22 NVR data with 78 textures. Two lanes, central jungle, altars, Vilemaw pit, speed shrine.

15 Champions

Real stats from LS4-3x3 server data. Annie, Garen, Ashe, Darius, Lux, Thresh, Jinx, Yasuo, Master Yi, Jax, Teemo, and more.

Combat System

Auto-attacks, abilities (Q/W/E/R), CC (stun/slow/root/silence), armor/MR penetration, kill bounties with real LoL formula.

Bot AI

5 bots (2 allies + 3 enemies) that lane, fight, retreat, and push objectives. Jungle camps with spawn timers and gold/XP rewards.

45 Items

Real item stats and prices. Doran's starters, boots, component items, and finished items for AD, AP, and Tank builds.

Objectives

Vilemaw epic boss, altar capture system with buffs, inhibitor destruction spawns super minions, nexus kill ends the game.

LoL Client UI

Menu styled like the real League of Legends client with Beaufort/Spiegel fonts, real backgrounds, and familiar layout.

Runes & Spells

5 rune paths, 15 keystones, 9 summoner spells (Flash, Ignite, Heal, etc.) with correct cooldowns and effects.

Open Source

MIT licensed. Built in Rust with Bevy 0.18. Modular crate architecture. Contributions welcome from the community.
